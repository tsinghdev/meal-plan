4Season with cumin, turmeric, paprika, salt & pepper.
5Bring to boil, reduce heat, simmer 30–35 min until lentils are tender.

1Preheat oven to 200°C / 400°F.
2Toss ~1.5kg chicken thighs with olive oil, paprika, oregano, salt & pepper.
3Chop seasonal veg (carrots, courgette, broccoli). Toss with olive oil & salt.
4Spread chicken and veg on a large baking tray.
5Roast 40–45 min until chicken is golden and cooked through (75°C internal).
6Rest 5 min before serving.
1Preheat oven to 190°C / 375°F.
